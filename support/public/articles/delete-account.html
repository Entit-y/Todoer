Deleting your account

Last updated May 2025

You can permanently delete your Todoer account from your profile page. This removes all your personal data from the system.

This cannot be undone Account deletion is permanent. Your tasks, workspaces you own, uploaded files, and account data will be deleted and cannot be recovered.

Before you delete

Consider the following before proceeding:

  • Workspaces you own — if you are the sole owner of any workspaces, those workspaces and all their contents will be deleted. Transfer ownership first if you want collaborators to retain access.
  • Shared workspaces — your tasks and contributions to workspaces you don't own will remain visible to other members after deletion, attributed to a deleted account.
  • Files — any files you uploaded will be permanently removed from workspaces you own. Files in shared workspaces will be removed too.

How to delete your account

  1. Sign in and go to your profile page
  2. Scroll to the bottom of the page
  3. Click Delete account
  4. Confirm by entering your password when prompted

Your account will be deleted immediately. You'll be signed out and redirected to the homepage.

Google accounts

If you signed up with Google and haven't set a password, you'll be asked to confirm deletion by signing in with Google one final time instead of entering a password.

Reactivating a deleted account

Deleted accounts cannot be reactivated. If you want to use Todoer again after deleting, you'll need to create a new account with the same or a different email address.
