Resetting your password

Last updated May 2025

If you've forgotten your password or can't sign in, you can request a password reset from the login page. The process takes under a minute.

Requesting a reset

  1. Go to entityy.site/forgot-password
  2. Enter the email address associated with your account
  3. Click Send reset link

You'll receive an email with a password reset link. The link is valid for 1 hour. If it expires, just request a new one.

Don't see the email? Check your spam or junk folder. If the email still hasn't arrived after a few minutes, make sure you're using the same email address you signed up with.

Setting a new password

  1. Click the link in the email — you'll be taken to the reset page
  2. Enter your new password and confirm it
  3. Click Reset password

You'll be signed in automatically after a successful reset.

Google accounts

If you signed up with Google, your Todoer account doesn't have a password — authentication is handled by Google. To change your Google password, visit your Google Account security settings.

Changing your password while signed in

If you're already signed in and want to update your password, go to your profile page and look for the Change password section. You'll need to enter your current password to confirm the change.

Still locked out?

If you no longer have access to the email address on your account, contact support and we'll help verify your identity and recover access.
