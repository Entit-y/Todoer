Connecting your Google account

Last updated May 2025

You can link a Google account to your Todoer account to enable one-click sign-in. Once connected, you can use either your password or Google to sign in.

Linking Google to an existing account

  1. Sign in to Todoer and go to your profile page
  2. Scroll to the Connected accounts section
  3. Click Connect Google
  4. Choose the Google account you want to link and grant permission

After linking, you'll be redirected back to your profile and the Google account will appear as connected.

Note The Google account you link must use the same email address as your Todoer account. Linking a Google account with a different email is not supported.

Signing in with Google

Once connected, the Continue with Google button on the sign-in page will work for your account. You won't need to enter your password — Google handles authentication.

Signing up with Google directly

If you don't have a Todoer account yet, you can create one using Google. Click Continue with Google on the registration page. Your display name and email will be pulled from your Google profile — no email verification step is required.

Disconnecting Google

Go to your profile page and click Disconnect next to the linked Google account. After disconnecting, you'll need to use your email and password to sign in.

Important If you signed up exclusively via Google and haven't set a password, disconnecting Google will lock you out of your account. Set a password first via the password reset flow before disconnecting.

Troubleshooting

  • The Google button doesn't work — try clearing your browser cookies and signing in again
  • "Email already in use" error — an account with that Google email already exists; sign in with your password and link from the profile page instead
  • Wrong Google account selected — sign out of Google in your browser, then try again
