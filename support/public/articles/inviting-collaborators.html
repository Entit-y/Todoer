Workspaces

Inviting collaborators to a workspace

Last updated May 2025

Workspaces support multiple members. You can invite anyone with a Todoer account — or send an invite to someone who hasn't signed up yet.

Sending an invitation

  1. Open the workspace you want to share
  2. Click the gear icon to open workspace settings
  3. Go to the Members tab
  4. Enter the email address of the person you want to invite and click Send invite

If the email belongs to an existing Todoer account, the invitation is sent directly. If not, they'll receive an email inviting them to create an account first — the workspace invite will be waiting for them after they verify their email.

Invitation link

Each workspace also has a shareable invite link. From the Members tab, click Copy invite link. Anyone who opens this link will be prompted to sign in or create an account, then added to the workspace as a member.

Note Invite links grant Member-level access. They cannot be used to add Owners. Rotate or disable the link from the Members tab if you no longer want it to work.

Pending invitations

Invitations that haven't been accepted yet appear under Pending invites in the Members tab. You can resend or revoke them at any time.

Removing a member

Owners can remove any member from the workspace. Go to the Members tab, find the person, and click Remove. Removed members immediately lose access to the workspace and its contents.

Leaving a workspace

Members can leave a workspace at any time from the Members tab by clicking Leave workspace. Workspace owners cannot leave — they must transfer ownership to another member first, or delete the workspace.
